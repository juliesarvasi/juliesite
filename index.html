About me

I'm a 24 years old Brazilian woman passionate about ideas, technology and people.

I graduated in Business Administration from Fundação Getulio Vargas (FGV-EAESP) with focus in sustainability. I discovered the product and technology world in 2017 working at EasyTaxi, a mobility startup in Brazil. I moved from project to product and started my technology carrer in 2018.

Working for 3 years at mobility startups, my passion for product continued to grow and I decided to start working as a Product Owner after a year working with Market Fit. I realized that being a Product Owner demands hard work and that I had gaps to fill and curiosity in Design and Technology, so I decided to take courses to improve my knowloge in these areas. This website was created during a Aluma Css workshop where I could learn more about front-end.
